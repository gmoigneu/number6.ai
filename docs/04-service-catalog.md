## Pricing Notes

**Free discovery call.** Every engagement starts with a free 30-minute call. We learn about your business, you learn about our approach, and we figure out if there's a fit. No pitch. No pressure.

**All prices in USD.** UK and European clients: we invoice in GBP or EUR at equivalent rates, adjusted quarterly.

**Payment terms.** Projects under $10K: 100% upfront. Projects $10K-$25K: 50% upfront, 50% at delivery. Projects over $25K: 40% upfront, 30% at midpoint, 30% at delivery. Retainers: monthly invoicing.

**Travel.** Virtual delivery is standard and included in all prices. In-person delivery available in Houston and Manchester metro areas at no extra cost. Other locations: travel expenses quoted separately.

**What's not included.** Third-party software licenses (we recommend, you subscribe). API costs for LLM usage in deployed solutions (typically $50-$500/month depending on volume — we help you estimate this). Any costs are discussed transparently before you commit.

**Satisfaction guarantee.** If you're not satisfied with a workshop or assessment after delivery, we'll redo it at no charge or refund your payment. For larger projects, satisfaction checkpoints are built into the payment schedule.
